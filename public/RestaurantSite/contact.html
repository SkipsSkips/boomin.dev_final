Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for dinner service and weekends. You can book a table online through our website or by calling us directly.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering for events of all sizes. Our catering menu features a variety of Greek specialties that can be customized to suit your needs. Please contact us for more information.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We offer a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform your server about any allergies or dietary restrictions, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate your needs.

Is there parking available?

We have a small parking lot behind our restaurant for customers. There is also street parking available and a public parking garage one block away.
