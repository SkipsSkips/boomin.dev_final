About Mythos

Discover the story behind our passion for authentic Greek cuisine and hospitality

Our Story

Mythos was born from a deep love for Greek culinary traditions and a desire to share the authentic flavors of the Mediterranean with our community. Our journey began when our founder, Nikos, decided to bring his family's treasured recipes from his small village in Greece to create a dining experience that captures the essence of Greek hospitality.

Since opening our doors in 2010, we have been dedicated to creating dishes that honor traditional Greek cooking methods while incorporating fresh, local ingredients. Every dish tells a story of Greek heritage, from our homemade tzatziki to our slow-roasted lamb.

At Mythos, we believe that a meal is more than just food—it's an experience to be shared and savored. Our restaurant is designed to transport you to the shores of the Mediterranean, with an atmosphere that combines rustic charm with modern elegance.

Our Values

Fresh Ingredients

We source the freshest ingredients from local farmers and import authentic Greek products to ensure the highest quality in every dish.

Traditional Recipes

Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the authentic flavors and cooking techniques of Greece.

Warm Hospitality

We treat every guest like family, embracing the Greek tradition of philoxenia—love of strangers and hospitality.

Sustainability

We are committed to sustainable practices, from reducing food waste to using eco-friendly packaging for takeout orders.
